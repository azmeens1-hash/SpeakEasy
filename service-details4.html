Curriculum

A Clear Path From A1 to C2

Six structured modules aligned to the CEFR framework, each building directly on the last.

01
Foundations — Greetings & Basics
4 weeks · 12 live sessions
A1
Core pronunciation, the alphabet, gender and articles, present tense of être/avoir, and everyday greetings.
  • Phonetics: nasal vowels, liaison, and accent basics
  • Numbers, dates, and telling time
  • Introducing yourself and basic small talk
02
Everyday Life
4 weeks · 12 live sessions
A2
Shopping, ordering food, asking directions, and describing your daily routine with confidence.
  • Present and near-future tense in conversation
  • Restaurant and café roleplay practice
  • Common idioms used in daily French life
03
Expressing Opinions
5 weeks · 15 live sessions
B1
Past tenses (passé composé, imparfait), debating everyday topics, and writing structured short texts.
  • Narrating past events fluently
  • Agreeing, disagreeing, and giving opinions
  • Reading short French news articles
04
Professional & Abstract French
5 weeks · 15 live sessions
B2
Subjunctive mood, conditional structures, and confident handling of abstract or hypothetical conversation.
  • Email and written correspondence etiquette
  • Workplace meetings and presentations
  • Understanding French radio and podcasts
05
Fluency & Nuance
6 weeks · 18 live sessions
C1
Idiomatic expression, register shifting between formal and casual French, and literary text analysis.
  • Reading excerpts from contemporary French literature
  • Debating complex social and cultural topics
  • Refining accent and intonation to near-native level
06
Mastery
6 weeks · 18 live sessions
C2
Near-native command of French across all registers — academic, professional, and creative writing.
  • Academic essay and argumentative writing
  • Simultaneous comprehension of fast native speech
  • Capstone project: a presentation entirely in French
★
DELF / DALF Exam Preparation
6 weeks · intensive · all levels
A1–C2
A focused track for anyone sitting the official DELF or DALF exam, run by certified examiners.
  • Full timed mock exams at your target level
  • Written and oral exam technique coaching
  • Marking-rubric breakdown so you know exactly what scores points
