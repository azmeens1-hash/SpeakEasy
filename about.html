Our Mission

Breaking Language Barriers

Language is the most powerful skill you can build. It opens careers, connects families, unlocks cultures, and builds bridges across borders. Yet most language education is slow, expensive, and ineffective — designed for classrooms, not for real lives.

SpeakEasy was built to fix that. We combined the irreplaceable magic of certified native human tutors with the 24/7 availability of AI-powered practice — and wrapped it in a curriculum that actually gets people fluent in 12 weeks.

“We don’t teach languages for certificates. We teach them for conversations — the ones that change your life.”
— Priya Krishnamurthy, Founder & CEO

Since 2015 we’ve grown from 3 tutors and 40 students in Hyderabad to a global academy with 50,000+ active learners across 12 languages and 80+ countries.

Our Values

What We Believe

These six values guide every lesson, every hire, and every decision we make.

01
Results Over Completion
We measure success by your fluency and exam scores — not by how many lessons you complete. If you don’t improve, we give your money back.
02
Human First, Always
AI powers our practice tools, but certified human tutors lead every class. Language is ultimately a human connection and we never forget that.
03
Accessible Education
World-class language education shouldn’t require a Western budget. Our pricing is designed for learners at every income level, everywhere.
04
Continuous Improvement
We analyse every lesson outcome to continuously improve our curriculum. Our pass rates go up every year because we never stop learning ourselves.
05
Small Groups Matter
We cap every group at 6 students. Because language learning requires speaking time — not silent observation in a 30-person classroom.
06
Science-Backed
Every method we use — spaced repetition, comprehensible input, pronunciation drilling — is backed by peer-reviewed language acquisition research.
