Course Structure

Spanish Week-by-Week Curriculum

12 weeks structured into 4 phases — each building directly on the last, from ¡Hola! to full fluency.

01
Week 1–2: Primeros Pasos (First Steps)
6 sessions · 3 hrs total
Foundation
▾
Spanish sounds, alphabet pronunciation, greetings, and essential phrases. Practice in simulated Madrid café and market scenarios.
  • Spanish phonetics: the rolled R, silent H, vowel sounds
  • Saludos: hola, buenos días, ¿cómo estás?
  • Numbers, time, and days of the week
  • Roleplay: ordering tapas, asking for directions in Madrid
02
Week 3: Conversación Cotidiana (Daily Conversation)
3 sessions · 1.5 hrs total
Foundation
▾
Introduce yourself, describe your life, and talk about your interests in Spanish.
  • Presente de indicativo: ser, estar, tener, hacer
  • Describing family, home, and daily routine
  • Spanish question words: qué, quién, cuándo, dónde, cómo
  • Likes and dislikes: me gusta / no me gusta
03
Week 4–5: Pretérito & Futuro (Past & Future)
6 sessions · 3 hrs total
Grammar
▾
Master the two key past tenses and the future — the tenses that unlock real storytelling in Spanish.
  • Pretérito indefinido: regular and irregular verbs
  • Pretérito imperfecto: childhood memories and descriptions
  • Futuro simple and ir a + infinitivo
  • Roleplay: narrating a trip to Barcelona, childhood memories
04
Week 6: Subjuntivo & Emociones
3 sessions · 1.5 hrs total
Grammar
▾
The Spanish subjunctive — the tense that separates intermediate learners from advanced ones.
  • Presente de subjuntivo: wishes, doubts, emotions
  • Quiero que..., Espero que..., Es posible que...
  • Expressing feelings: estoy feliz de que, me molesta que
05
Week 7: España y Latinoamérica en la Vida Real
6 sessions · 3 hrs total
Immersion
▾
Navigate real-world Spanish scenarios — from airport emergencies to business meetings in Mexico City.
  • Travel: airports, hotels, emergencies in Spanish
  • Healthcare: describing symptoms to a Spanish doctor
  • Work: meeting colleagues, presenting ideas in Spanish
  • Spain vs Latin America: key vocabulary & accent differences
06
Week 9: Modismos, Jerga y Cultura (Idioms, Slang & Culture)
3 sessions · 1.5 hrs total
Immersion
▾
Sound authentically Spanish — the phrases, humour, and cultural nuance textbooks never teach.
  • 50 essential Spanish idioms: estar en las nubes, tener mala leche...
  • Spanish slang: tío/tía, guay, chulo, mola
  • La siesta, las fiestas, and Spanish social norms
  • Vosotros vs ustedes: Spain vs Latin America register
07
Week 10–11: Sprints de Fluidez (Fluency Sprints)
6 sessions · 3 hrs total
Advanced
▾
High-intensity Spanish conversation practice — debates, storytelling, roleplay under pressure.
  • 30-second improvised Spanish speech drills
  • Debate topics: política, medio ambiente, cultura
  • Full vocabulary review: 1,200+ Spanish words
  • AI pronunciation coaching: perfecting your Spanish accent
08
Week 12: Evaluación Final & Certificado DELE
3 sessions · 1.5 hrs total
DELE Certification
▾
A 20-minute live Spanish conversation with a DELE-certified examiner + your personalised fluency report and CEFR certificate.
  • Mock DELE oral exam in Week 11 session 3
  • Personalised Spanish fluency feedback report
  • CEFR-aligned digital certificate (PDF + LinkedIn)
  • DELE exam registration guidance and discount code
