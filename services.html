What Makes Us Different

Every feature we build exists for one reason: to help you reach fluency faster and enjoy the process more.

Adaptive AI Lessons
Our engine analyses your responses in real-time and adjusts vocabulary, speed, and grammar complexity so every lesson is calibrated to your exact level.
Native Cultural Context
We embed idioms, cultural references, and real-world scenarios so you don't just learn the language — you understand the world that speaks it.
Multi-Device Access
Seamlessly switch between desktop, tablet, and mobile. Your progress, notes, and lesson history sync automatically across every device you own.
AI Pronunciation Coach
Our speech-recognition AI listens to your pronunciation, gives a score, and pinpoints exactly which sounds need work — with audio comparison from a native speaker.
Live Progress Dashboard
Track your CEFR level, vocabulary bank, streak, XP points, and weekly learning hours in a beautiful real-time dashboard available on all plans.
Gamified Milestones
Earn badges, complete weekly challenges, and unlock achievement streaks. Language learning is more effective — and far more fun — when it feels like a game.
Live Group Classes
Small groups of 6–10 learners mean you get real speaking practice, peer interaction, and the energy of a classroom — from anywhere in the world.
Spaced Repetition System
Our flashcard engine uses scientifically proven spaced repetition to show you words at exactly the right time — permanently building your vocabulary bank.
Flexible Scheduling
Book sessions in 30-minute windows across morning, afternoon, and evening slots — 7 days a week. All sessions auto-converted to your local timezone.
